Steve Harvey

About

Steve Harvey is a popular American actor, producer, comedian, and television host.

Steve Harvey started as a stand-up comedian in the early 1980's before moving to television.

He is most well known for his role as the host of the popular American TV show Family Feud.

In addition to this, he also hosted Showtime at the Apollo and the Steve Harvey Show.

Harvey has also written four books, including his bestseller Act Like a Lady, Think Like a Man, which was published in March 2009.

In 2017, Harvey founded Steve Harvey Global, an entertainment company that houses his production company East 112 and various other ventures.

He and his wife Marjorie are the founders of The Steve and Marjorie Harvey Foundation, a nonprofit organization focused on youth education.
